There are no strangers here

Only friends you have yet to meet


Our Mission is to provide a congenial and happy environment for seniors to enjoy activities, meet new friends, and attend educational programs. Furthermore, to provide a resource for obtaining assistance and guidance in times of need.

WELCOME TO YOUR HOME AWAY FROM HOME

We invite you to check out how WPSC can provide the ways to enrich, expand and maintain an active lifestyle while having fun, making friends and learning new things. You are a click away from exploring and immersing yourself in those new, exciting activities you've always wanted to try!
